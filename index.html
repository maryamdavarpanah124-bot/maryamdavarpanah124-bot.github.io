Short English Bio

Maryam Davarpanah is a mathematics instructor specializing in Differential Equations, Calculus (General Mathematics 1, 2, and 3), Linear Algebra, and Real Analysis. She holds an M.Sc. in Pure Mathematics from Ferdowsi University of Mashhad and has been teaching at universities and academic institutes in Iran since 2005.

Her video courses, published on Faradars and NikDars Academy, are known as some of the most comprehensive Persian resources in their fields, featuring structured explanations, conceptual depth, and a large number of carefully selected examples. All courses have received 5-star satisfaction ratings from learners.
